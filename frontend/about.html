Background

Environmental hazards (air/water pollution, waste dumps) are often unreported due to lack of structured mechanisms.

Expected Solution

Build a platform for reporting with images/geotags, validation by experts/volunteers, and open-source remediation plans.

Our Go-To-Strategy

  • Strategy One: Website - Web based platform
  • Strategy Two: Application - App based for Android and iOS

Our Big Idea

  • Public can lodge complaints, collaborative community, open source plans, increased accountability.
  • Government Action: Official complaints, action based on urgency, proof of elimination.
  • Open Source Remediation Plans: Unique plans, public contribution, voting-based.
  • Points Based System: More contributions = more points, level up, validate others, urgency list.

Methodology

  1. Reporting: Submit with images + geotags.
  2. Data Validation: AI filters spam, classifies type (air, water, waste).
  3. Visualization: Interactive maps (heatmaps + timelines).
  4. Action Plans: Generate suggestions (clean-up, NGO, alerts).
  5. Feedback & Tracking: Update status, track progress.

Future Scope & Scalability

  • IoT Integration: Sensors for live data.
  • Satellite & Drone Data: Cross-verify reports.
  • Community Partnerships: With schools/NGOs.
  • Policy Expansion: Dashboards for governments/SDG reporting.
