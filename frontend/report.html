Hazard Categories

🌋
Natural Hazards
  • Earthquakes
  • Tsunamis
  • Volcanic eruptions
  • Landslides & rockfalls
  • Floods (river floods, flash floods, urban floods)
  • Cyclones / Hurricanes / Typhoons
  • Droughts
  • Heatwaves & cold waves
  • Wildfires / Forest fires
  • Avalanches
  • Extreme storms (hailstorms, thunderstorms, lightning strikes)
🏭
Technological & Industrial Hazards
  • Nuclear/radiation accidents
  • Industrial/chemical disasters (e.g., gas leaks, explosions)
  • Structural collapse (dams, bridges, buildings)
  • Power failures & electrical hazards
  • Transportation accidents (road, rail, air, maritime crashes)
  • Oil spills & tanker accidents
  • Mining accidents (explosions, cave-ins, toxic leaks)
🌱
Environmental / Ecological Hazards
  • Air pollution (smog, toxic emissions)
  • Water pollution (contamination, algal blooms, chemical spills)
  • Soil degradation & contamination (pesticides, heavy metals)
  • Deforestation & desertification
  • Biodiversity loss & habitat destruction
  • Climate change impacts (sea level rise, glacier melt, extreme weather)
🧍
Health & Biological Hazards
  • Epidemics (cholera, malaria, dengue, etc.)
  • Pandemics (COVID-19, influenza)
  • Food insecurity & malnutrition
  • Zoonotic diseases (animal-to-human diseases like bird flu, rabies)
🏢
Social & Human-Induced Hazards
  • Terrorism & armed conflicts
  • Riots & civil unrest
  • Refugee crises / mass displacement
  • Cyber hazards (data breaches, infrastructure hacking)
  • Urban crowding / slum conditions
⚒️
Occupational Hazards
  • Workplace accidents (falls, machinery accidents)
  • Chemical exposure (pesticides, solvents, asbestos)
  • Physical hazards (noise, vibration, extreme temperatures)
  • Ergonomic hazards (repetitive strain, poor posture)
  • Biological exposure (healthcare workers, lab workers at risk)
